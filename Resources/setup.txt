This is a setup file.
First and second value will indicate number of parts the image shall be split to (horizontal and vertical in that order).
Both values given must be both positive and whole numbers for the program to work properly. Zeros are not allowed.
Third and last variable should be the name of image you want to use and its extension.
Note that for the best image display its resolution should not be lower than your current screen resolution and their proportions as close to each other as possible.
